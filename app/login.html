  • High carbon footprint from manufacturing new products
  • No structured system to incentivise reuse on campus
Logistical & Safety Issues
  • Inefficient platforms like OLX and Facebook groups
  • High risk of scams and fraudulent listings
  • Privacy and safety concerns when meeting strangers
